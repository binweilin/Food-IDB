Bio

Gordon James Ramsay, Jr. /ˈræm.ziː/, OBE (born 8 November 1966) is a Scottish celebrity chef, restaurateur, and television personality.[1] His restaurants have been awarded 15 Michelin stars in total and currently hold 14.[2][3][4] His signature restaurant, Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea, London has held 3 Michelin stars since 2001. Ramsay is known for presenting TV programmes about competitive cookery and food, such as the British series Hell's Kitchen, The F Word, and Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares, along with the American versions of Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, MasterChef, and Masterchef Junior.
